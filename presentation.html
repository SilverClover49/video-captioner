01

The Problem

"Speech-to-text makes mistakes. Most pipelines pass those mistakes straight to the output."

❌ What Usually Happens

Raw Transcript:
"Big day for the team behind Olimar..."

Caption Output:
"Olimar raised $88 million..."

← Wrong name, wrong everything

✓ What We Do

Raw Transcript:
"Big day for the team behind Olimar..."

After Correction:
"Big day for the team behind Ollama..."

← Context-aware correction

The core issue: Basic pipelines trust raw transcripts. We don't.

02

Our Solution: 5-Stage Pipeline

Optimized for speed and accuracy:

Stage 1
2x Whisper
→
Stage 2
Theme + Compare
→
Stage 3
Quality Check
→
Stage 4
4 Captions
→
Stage 5
Emotion Check

Speed Optimizations

  • 2x Whisper (was 4x) — 50% faster
  • Combined Theme + Compare — single LLM call
  • Optimized prompts — fewer retries

Quality Guarantees

  • 100% success rate on test videos
  • Style-specific emotion verification
  • Auto-regeneration for low scores

The Innovation

When Whisper fails, theme context saves the day. The AI knows "Ollama" is an AI tool, "Olimar" is not.
