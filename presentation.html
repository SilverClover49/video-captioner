01

The Problem

"Speech-to-text makes mistakes. Most pipelines pass those mistakes straight to the output."

❌ What Usually Happens

Raw Transcript:
"Big day for the team behind Olimar..."

Caption Output:
"Olimar raised $88 million..."

← Wrong name, wrong everything

✓ What We Do

Raw Transcript:
"Big day for the team behind Olimar..."

After Stage 2:
"Big day for the team behind Ollama..."

← Corrected before captioning

The core issue: Basic pipelines trust raw transcripts. We don't.

02

Our Solution: 5-Stage Pipeline

Unlike naive approaches, we validate at every step:

Stage 1
Transcribe
→
Stage 2
Correct Errors
→
Stage 3
3 Draft Captions
→
Stage 4
Cross-Check
→
Stage 5
4 Style Outputs

Stage 2: Error Correction

  • Catches wrong names (Olimar → Ollama)
  • Fixes technical terms (silicon → silicone)
  • Corrects numbers and figures

Stage 4: Cross-Validation

  • Compares 3 different drafts
  • Identifies discrepancies
  • Produces verified master caption
